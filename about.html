Meet Our Founder

Michelle "Shell" Payne, DPT

Founder & Physical Therapist

Doctor of Physical Therapy from Texas Woman's University. With years of experience in pediatric and adult therapy, Shell founded Play with a Purpose to provide specialized, individualized care in Greenville, TX.

Her passion is helping children build the skills they need for the job of living, and empowering adults to break free from sedentary lifestyles through targeted movement therapy.
