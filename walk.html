About Orthotics

Orthotics are supported devices worn of the body to help improve alignment, stability, and movement. They are often placed inside shoes or worn around the foot and ankle to support muscles and joints.

Orthotics can help:

✔ Improve balance and coordination

✔ Reduce pain and fatigue

✔ Support weak or unstable joints

✔ Prevent worsening of foot or ankle alignment

✔ Help children and adults move more efficiently
