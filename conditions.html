Conditions

We specialize in treating children from birth to 21 years of age with a wide variety of diagnosis.

Conditions We Treat

Developmental Delays

Children who are not meeting expected developmental milestones in gross motor skills, balance, coordination, or strength.

Sensory Processing Disorder

Difficulty processing and responding to sensory information, affecting movement, behavior, and learning.

Neurological & Genetic Conditions

Including cerebral palsy, Down syndrome, autism spectrum disorders, and other conditions affecting movement and development.
